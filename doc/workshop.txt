[b]花枪 Trick Spear[/b] — 花式舞矛，灵感来自 [i]Trick Saber[/i]。

[img]https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/esphas/TrickSpear/static/preview01.gif[/img]
[url=https://github.com/esphas/TrickSpear]Github[/url]

需先在键位设置（Improved Input Config）中配置花枪键，默认键盘 [b]Q[/b]、手柄 [b]LB[/b]。持矛时按下花枪键施展动作，长按可持续施展。

[b]功能选项[/b]
[list]
[*][b]自动花枪[/b] — 在特定行动时自动施展一次，目前支持：滑行、快速转身、翻滚、后空翻。
[*][b][战斗] 小型物体交互[/b] — 旋转时，矛头可击落悬挂物、扫开轻型物体。
[*][b][战斗] 格挡[/b] — 每次旋转开始时，可格挡并弹开远程攻击。默认判定窗口 5 帧。
[/list]

[b]联机兼容性（雨甸 Rain Meadow）[/b]
[list]
[*][strike]玩家不必都安装本模组；未安装者看不到花枪动画，矛的位置也可能略显怪异。[/strike] 据反馈，联机时如果此模组安装情况不一致可能导致不同步。
[*]联机模式下，无论配置如何，花枪的战斗选项（物体交互、格挡）均 [b]禁用[/b]。
[/list]

联机相关功能测试仍不充分，欢迎积极反馈。

--- en

[b]Trick Spear[/b] — flashy spear twirling inspired by [i]Trick Saber[/i].

[img]https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/esphas/TrickSpear/static/preview01.gif[/img]
[url=https://github.com/esphas/TrickSpear]Github[/url]

Assign the twirl key in Improved Input Config first (default: [b]Q[/b] on keyboard, [b]LB[/b] on gamepad). Press it while holding a spear to twirl; hold the button to keep going.

[b]Options[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Auto twirl[/b] — automatically twirl once during certain moves: belly slide, turn, roll, and backflip.
[*][b][Combat] Small object interactions[/b] — while the spear tip spins, knock down hanging items and sweep aside light objects.
[*][b][Combat] Parry[/b] — at the start of each spin, block and deflect ranged attacks. Default parry window: 5 frames.
[/list]

[b]Rain Meadow (multiplayer)[/b]
[list]
[*][strike]Not everyone needs this mod installed; players without it will not see your twirl animation, and spear placement may look off on their screen.[/strike] Per player feedback, mismatched install status in multiplayer may cause desync.
[*]In online play, combat options (object interactions and parry) are [b]disabled[/b] regardless of your settings.
[/list]

Online support is still early — feedback is welcome.
